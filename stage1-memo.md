The Risk
Our primary risk is EUR depreciation. If the euro weakens over the next year, the USD value of our €8M receivable declines. Given ongoing uncertainty in the ECB policy and geopolitical factors. The EURUSD volatility remains a material concern. Without a hedge, our future USD is fully exposed to adverse currency movements.

The "Forward Contract" locks in a guaranteed USD amount at 1.0890; no upfront cost; simple to execute, but eliminates upside if EUR strengthens; fully binding.
The "Money Market Hedge" uses borrowing/lending to synthetically lock in a rate; may outperform the forward depending on interest‑rate differentials, however perationally more complex; requires balance‑sheet usage and credit capacity.
Options (EUR Put / EUR Call) provides downside protection while preserving upside potential; flexible payoff structure and requires paying premiums (Put: $0.021; Call: $0.026); more expensive than forwards.
